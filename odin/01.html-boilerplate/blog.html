How to make a Water Cake

First of all, you need to gather all the necessary ingredients. They are really just water.

Secondly, you need to follow every step with close attention. Boil the water until it reaches 100 celsius degrees. This is very important! Then put it on a cake recipient that has the desired format. Now you need to let the cake adquired its taste for 10 minutes. After that, you can put the cake in the refrigerator to cool down. That's it, now you have a water cake.
